About us

Who we are

Hometree is an industry leading provider of home care plans across the UK. We have created care plans which cover the repair or replacement of our customers home’s gas appliances and systems.

We are different

We believe that home care has been broken for too long. By redefining how customers interact with their homes through technology and world-class customer service, we have the tools to fix home care for good. Everything we do is based on feedback from our customers, which is why we are the best-rated home care provider in the market.

We talk to you like a friend

You can trust us to be very clear and friendly when we communicate with you. You will always know exactly what you are paying for and we will never throw jargon at you, especially not in our terms and conditions.

No more price hikes

We understand how frustrating price changes can be even if you haven’t arranged a call out during the year. We promise that we’ll never raise your monthly or annual prices by more than inflation if you haven't had a call out.

The Hometree Story

We’ve all been there. You wake up and you have no heating and hot water. You then spend an hour on hold and then finally get through to a customer service rep that can’t find your details. You then have to wait several days for an engineer visit and are only offered an all-day slot so you have to book the day off work. Finally, on the day, they call you 30 mins before the end of the day saying that they won’t make it. You are so frustrated with your home care provider and then get that renewal letter through. 30% price hike!? Grrrrr.

Home care is broken and we want to fix it. Our mission is to fix a large industry which is lacking in transparency, providing a terrible customer experience and raising prices year after year. We're doing things differently.

We want to build a company with our customers in mind. By constantly learning and listening to our customers, we are able to build new products and features built for everyone in mind.

Due to our customer-first approach, we are the best rated Trustpilot provider of home care plans in the UK. We do all the things customers love. We are more comprehensive than our competitors, our hold times are up to 30 seconds, we never unfairly hike prices and we do it all with the happiest and friendliest customer service staff and engineers.
